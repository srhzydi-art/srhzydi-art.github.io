FFB Ripening Dynamics in Malaysia

Field: Crop Physiology & Food Security

In Malaysian oil palm plantations, determining the exact moment of peak oil accumulation is vital for food security. We model the weight of a **Fresh Fruit Bunch (FFB)** using a Logistic Growth Function.

Growth Function: $f(x) = \frac{L}{1 + e^{-k(x-x_0)}}$
Derivative (Rate of Change): $f'(x) = k \cdot f(x) \cdot (1 - \frac{f(x)}{L})$

Where: $L=25kg$ (Max weight), $k=0.15$ (Growth rate), $x_0=30$ (Midpoint days).
